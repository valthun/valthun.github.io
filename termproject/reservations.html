Rafting or Kayaking?

Book it Now

Note we are seasonal and run the river between May 15 to September 15.

We may have to cancel based on weather and river conditions. But mostly we run rain or shine.

Note: Private groups requires 4 adventurers. We will team you up if you don't have enough people.

Boat, Oars and are included in the fees

For half day adventures please pack your own snacks and water.

For 1 day and 2 day adventures we will provide a lunch, 2 days also include a dinner and breakfast. Please let us know of any dietary restrictions.
