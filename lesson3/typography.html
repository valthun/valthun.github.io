For the body I have used a serif style font named "Crete Round". The reason for this is that generally a serif style font is easier to ready and is used in most body copy fonts. The look of this font is traditional in nature but has a simple modern look to it.

For the headers and title font I have gone with a sans-serif font title "Yanone Kaffeesatz". This font provides a nice contrast and will allow titles of articles stand out.
